    PARENT
    RESOURCES

    The Parent Resources are designed for parents and children to have conversations about resiliency at home. For example, when discussing mentorship in the home, the parent resource for grades six through eight provides suggestions to help the child find a mentor and two activities the parents can complete with their child. These resources focus on the Resiliency Characteristics by providing opportunities to continue discussions around Resiliency Education.
